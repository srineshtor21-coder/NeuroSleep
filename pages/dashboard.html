Research Interpretation

These charts implement the core analytic framework of NeuroSleep: matching individual behavioral data against published sleep neuroscience to generate personalized, research-backed behavioral guidance.

Correlation strength improves substantially with more data points. Log sleep for at least 14 consecutive days before drawing conclusions from your scatter plots.
Note on Causality: Correlations in this dashboard show association, not causation. Sleep improvements improve cognition, but cognitive test scores also vary with motivation, practice effects, and time of day. Interpret trends rather than single data points.

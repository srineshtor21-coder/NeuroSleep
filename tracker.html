💡 Sleep Optimization Tips

☀️
Morning Light Exposure

Get 10-30 minutes of bright outdoor light within 30 minutes of waking. This anchors your circadian clock and promotes earlier, more consistent sleep onset.

📷
Screen Cutoff Timing

Eliminate blue light exposure at least 60-90 minutes before bed. Use night mode or blue-light blocking glasses in the evening to preserve melatonin onset.

🏧
Temperature Regulation

A cool bedroom (65-68°F / 18-20°C) facilitates the core body temperature drop needed to initiate and maintain deep slow-wave sleep.

🕐
Consistent Schedule

Maintaining a fixed wake time even on weekends is the single most powerful intervention for improving sleep consistency and circadian alignment.
